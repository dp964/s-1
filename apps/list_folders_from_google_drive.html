LIST FOLDERS FROM GOOGLE DRIVE

About this app

This app was created to display folders from Google Drive :
  1. Retrieve list of folders and files from Google Drive
  2. Render HTML and post-processing

Launch App

To launch "List Files from Google Drive", you can launch it here,
(optional: enter Google Drive folder id)

Launch App

Custom Configurations

The script can be configured by adding parameters to the script url as follows

?serve: the encrypted id of the folder in Google Drive
  drive: Specifies the ID is not encrypted

?folder: the id of the folder in Google Drive, requires "serve" set to "drive"

Privacy Policy & Terms of Service:

Users upon granting access to the script, grant access to any Google Drive folders & files that can be retrieved by the user.

Users email address and file content may be displayed but are not stored remotely. No information is retained or disclosed to any other user or third-party organisation.
