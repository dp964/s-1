Constructing the 4 digit THSC code

The first 3 digits corresponds to the subject and the last digit corresponds to the type of paper.

Paper Types
0 - HSC paper or general files
1 - 1st assessment task CT1 or PT1
2 - 2nd assessment task CT2 or PT2
3 - 3rd assessment task CT3
4 - 4th assessment task CT4
5 - competition papers
6 - half yearly exam
7 - reserved
8 - yearly exam or trial exams
